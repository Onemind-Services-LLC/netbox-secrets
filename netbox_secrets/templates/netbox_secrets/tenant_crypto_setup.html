How It Works
  1. Create Passkey: Your device generates a unique cryptographic key pair.
  2. Derive Keys: A tenant encryption key is generated and encrypted to your public key.
  3. Access Secrets: When you need to decrypt a secret, your Passkey unlocks the tenant key.
  4. Zero Knowledge: The server only stores encrypted data - it never sees your keys or plaintext secrets.
